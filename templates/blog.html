Getting Started With Angular

Step 1: Bootstrapping

The ng-app directive is used as an attribute on an element to tell angular what portion of the markup should be treated as an Angular application.

Step 2: Expressions

Expressions in Angular are evaluated inside double curly braces and are replaced with the value of the expression.

Step 3: Controllers

Controllers are used in Angular to perform logic on a scoped portion of the markup

Step 4: Model and Two Way Data Binding

ngModel is used in Angular for the use of two-way data binding.

Step 5: ngRepeat

ngRepeat is used in Angular to loop over arrays and output data to the view.

Step 6: Directives

Directives are portions of markup that can be ajaxed into a page

Step 7: ngRoute

ngRoute enables the ability to serve templates and controllers based on the route.
